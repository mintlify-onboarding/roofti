---

In order to get started with our Mekari Jurnal integration, you will need to first create your Mekari Jurnal application. After that, you will need to enter your Client ID and Client Secret on the RootFi dashboard.

These steps are elaborated upon in detail below.

  
---

### Part 1: Get Credentials

To get Mekari Jurnal credentials, you have to contact Mekari Jurnal ([https://developer.jurnal.id/](https://developer.jurnal.id/)), create a new app and copy the client id and client secret from the credentials tab. To get started quickly, you can use RootFi credentials (client id and client secret).

  
### Part 2: Enter Your Client ID and Client Secret on the RootFi Dashboard

1. Login to the RootFi dashboard
2. Select the 'Integrations' tab on the sidebar - here you will see a list of platforms you can integrate with
3. Click the 'Set Up' button which is present on the Mekari Jurnal card.
4. When the form opens up simply enter the 'Client ID' and 'Client Secret' which you got from Mekari Jurnal and click 'Save'.
