Authorization is a crucial part of RootFi Integrate - every customer must authorize access to their data before you can pull that data. A frictionless and secure authorization flow is essential for accessing your customers' data.

## Intro to RootFi Connect

RootFi Connect streamlines the process of connecting your application to various accounting platforms, providing two efficient methods for integration: RootFi Link and RootFi SDK. These solutions are designed to cater to different use cases and requirements, ensuring seamless integration and data access.

  
### RootFi Connect Link: No-Code User Integrations

RootFi Connect Link is a no-code solution that enables you to connect your end users to accounting platforms without writing any frontend code. This approach delivers a smooth, in-browser experience and simplifies the integration process.

#### Key features of RootFi Connect Link:

* Easy to implement: Simply generate a unique URL, and your users can click the link to connect their accounting platform to your application.
* Customizable: Personalize the connection process by modifying the appearance and behavior of the link.
* Secure: RootFi Connect Link ensures secure connections with end-to-end encryption and adherence to industry standards.

### RootFi Connect SDK: Frontend Integration with Flexibility

RootFi Connect SDK is a software development kit that provides a more customizable and flexible white-labelled solution for integrating accounting platforms into your application optimized for conversion and building trust. With RootFi Connect SDK, you have full control over the user interface and can tailor the integration process to fit your specific needs.

#### Key features of RootFi Connect SDK:

* Highly customizable: Skip configuration screens but providing the information upfront, add your logo and choose how to handle success and error flows to match your application's requirements and design.
* Robust documentation: Access comprehensive documentation to guide you through the integration process.
* Seamless integration: RootFi Connect SDK is designed to work smoothly with your existing tech stack, making it easy to incorporate into your application.
