| Term       | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Platform   | Refers to the accounting software, commerce platform, or payment processor used by your Customer. (Eg. - Zoho books, Amazon, etc.)                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
