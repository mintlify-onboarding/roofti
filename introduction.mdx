## What is RootFi?

RootFi makes accessing business data from your customers' accounting, and commerce platforms easy. Build once to our API to aggregate data, underwrite credit risk, and automate accounting for your business customers.

By providing real-time connectivity, we enable you to focus on your core product instead of wasting tech bandwidth on building and maintaining integrations.

Visit our website [rootfi.dev](https://bit.ly/3ulLF5r) to learn more. From our website, you can also reach out to us by opening up a chat from the bottom right of the page
